the product :

Vote is a social network designed to help people participate in the political life of the city. The product is conceived as a state tool for overcoming the barrier between the government and the population, bypassing the media, creating a common decision-making initiative.

the problem :

Communication between the government and the population has historically been implemented through the media. This strategy often fails and does not shorten the distance of both sides. The political sphere affects the political and social activists of society, while involvement should extend to all members of the urban community.

the goal :

Design an app that will remove the communication barrier and create a single platform for communication, making decisions and creating news.

my role :

UX designer leading the app and responsive website design from conception to delivery: conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.
okey, problem's defined, let's start empathize with the user

user research : summary

After conducting a series of interviews and analyzing the data obtained, the following group was identified: broad group from young adults (20s) to elder adults. Since the problem is extensive and affects all residents of the city (country), the layers of society involved are extensive. The research involved people of different ages and social status.

usability study : pain points

Reliability :
People doubt the reliability of news information;
Transparency :
Citizens have a poor understanding of the daily and global activities of the government;
Results :
People in society do not see the changes behind their own introduced actions such as voting.

persona 1: alexander

Alexander is a law faculty student and a social activist who needs to getverified news about social and political city events because political and social decisions are make without taking into account the opinion of citizens as they are not aware of these processes.

persona 2: viktoria

Viktoria is a history Ph D, retiree and a grandmother who needs to know about political and social city events and take part in it because it makes her feel that she’s not alone and she benefits society.

ideation

During ideation process I paid attention to accessibility and optimization because the problem and users have a wide range. Main questions were: how to make basic functions immediately understandable and intuitive for users? How to impement new functions (ex.: voting) into the interface without losing the visual familiar to the user?

usability study : findings

Action possibility :
Users feel more comfortable with ability to do usual actions like: like, save, share;
Direct search :
Users choose different ways for informational search. Direct - is one of the main types. It must be provided;
Certainty :
Users are frustrated when can't be sure about buttons actions. Decision here: labaling and usual intuitive image icons for visual support.

accessibility considerations

  1. Screen readers friendly labeling of navigation icons;
  2. Colors match WCAG ratio color principals for users with visual disabilities;
  3. Images alt text for screen readers.
responsive design

sitemap

The main target was creation of a clear and intuitive logical user flow. Several structures were mixed: database and hierarchial to achieve the target.

responsive designs

Responsive design was created according to Progressive Enhancement system (mobile first).

During development principals were used:

  1. consistency;
  2. context;
  3. complementary.

impact :
Users experience a pleasant surprise during the flow.
